About Coral Reefs

Coral Reef Biodiversity

Coral reefs are teeming with life. They are known as the rainforests of the sea with how many various species live within their waters. The defining feature of these ecosystems are the plant-like coral, which are really colonies of tiny invertebrates called polyps.

Sadly, many reefs around the world are in danger due to rising ocean temperatures, pollution, and overfishing.
